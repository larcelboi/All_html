Levi Ackerman (Attack on Titan) Gojo Satoru
  • Anime: Attack on Titan
  • Role: Captain of the Special Operations Squad (aka Levi Squad)
  • Abilities: Exceptional speed, agility, and skill with ODM gear; considered humanity’s strongest soldier
  • Personality: Stoic, disciplined, and brutally efficient in combat. Has a strong sense of justice and loyalty, especially toward comrades.
  • Trivia: Despite his cold demeanor, Levi is a clean freak and has a surprisingly dry sense of humor.
